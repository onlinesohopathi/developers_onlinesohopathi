  • Q711.Nrb Araf asked:

    Is there any difference between paste and gel?

    SSC1I don't know
    Subject: I don't know
    1 answers
    Shadman Majid replied:

    Gel is an apparently solid,jelly-like material formed from a colloidal solution,by weight,gels are mostly liquid,yet they behave like solids,an example is gelatin. On the other hand, in physics,a paste is a substance that behaves as a solid until a sufficiently large load or stress is applied, at which point it flows like a fluid, It consists of a suspension of small particles in a background fluid.

    Shadab Raihan replied:

    The Binomial Series is the expansion (1 + x) n = 1 + nx + n(n − 1)*( x^ 2)/2!+ n(n − 1)(n − 2) *(x^ 3)/3! + . . . which is valid for any number n, positive or negative, integer or fractional, provided that −1 < x < 1.
